About devopX

We're the team of experts your company needs to take it to the next level.

Our main goal is to make infrastructure simple, reliable and scalable. In order to do so we focus on your technology needs and design a custom made plan to achieve your business goals.

Whether you need to migrate a server between providers, set up a high availability cluster in Amazon Web Services or even create your own cloud, we have done it and will help you to get it right.

Some of our key values:

  • We focus on your success
  • We love what we do
  • We are a team of highly experienced sysadmins/devops
  • We have +100 customers in +10 countries
  • We enjoy working on technical challenges, have one? Bring it on!
